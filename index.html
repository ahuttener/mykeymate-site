How it works

From sign-up to match in three steps.

We don't drop you into an endless list. First we learn who you are, then we show only what actually fits.

Step 01

Vibe check

Six quick questions: routine, tidiness, sleep schedule, smoking, pets and how social you like home to be.

Step 02

Smart matching

We cross your profile with rooms and roommates that fit — and show you why you click, not just a price.

Step 03

Chat with hosts inside the app. Your number and details stay private until you decide to share them.
